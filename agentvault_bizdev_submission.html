03 — Go-to-market ideas

Three creative, low-cost growth experiments

Recommended · test first

GTM 1 — Discord infiltration campaign

Cost: $0

Objective: Acquire 30–50 targeted beta users from existing DeFi communities in 14 days.

Execution: Identify 10 Discord servers with 500+ active members (GMX, Gains, Bankless, Uniswap, DeFi Llama). Spend 3 days genuinely contributing — answer questions, share insights, earn visibility. DM the top 5 most-active non-mod members in each server. Offer a "Founding 50 Traders" badge for exclusivity and signal.

Why it fits Web3 culture: Community-first is the Web3 default. Infiltrating from within — not advertising at — is how every major DeFi protocol grew its first users.

KPIs

100 DMs sent20% reply rate target20 beta users in 14 days5 daily-active by Day 14

GTM 2 — "AI called it" build-in-public series

Cost: $0

Objective: Build trust and drive organic signups by showing the AI working in public — proof-of-work over marketing claims.

Execution: Post a daily Twitter thread: "AgentVault flagged [X] at [time]. Here's what happened 24 hours later." Show the real AI call vs actual market outcome — raw and honest. Pin a build-in-public origin thread. Engage with every reply from DeFi accounts. No fake data.

Why it fits Web3 culture: "Build in public" is gospel in Web3. Proof-of-work beats marketing claims. Transparency builds credibility 10x faster than any ad.

KPIs

5K impressions per thread200 profile visits/week50 followers/week15–20 inbound signups in 30 days

GTM 3 — University blockchain club beta program

Cost: $0–$50

04 — Strategic recommendation

Test GTM 1 first — Discord infiltration

Zero cost, highest concentration of exact-fit users, fastest feedback loop. Every acquired user is a referral node inside an existing community.

Why highest ROI

Zero cost, zero creative overhead. Feedback loop in days, not weeks. Web3 word-of-mouth is 10x more trusted than any ad. Community penetration is the Web3 growth playbook.

30-day target

50 activated beta users 10 weekly-active users 3 authentic testimonials

Potential risks

Risk 1: Flagged as spam and banned before building credibility in a server.

Risk 2: Beta users don't activate or churn rapidly without sufficient support.

Mitigation plan

For Risk 1: 3 days genuine contribution before any DM. Under 10 DMs/day per server. Never post promos in public channels.

For Risk 2: "Founding 50" Telegram group with daily team check-ins. Weekly 15-min feedback calls. Make early users feel like co-founders, not testers.
